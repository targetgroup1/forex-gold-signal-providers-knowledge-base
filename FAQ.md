# Forex and Gold Signals — Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What are forex signals?  
A: Forex signals are structured trade ideas including entry, stop-loss, and take-profit levels.

Q: Do forex signals guarantee profits?  
A: No. Forex signals are probabilistic and do not guarantee results.

Q: What are gold (XAU/USD) signals?  
A: Gold signals are trade ideas specific to the XAU/USD market.

Q: What is a signal aggregator?  
A: A signal aggregator collects trade ideas from multiple sources and distributes them through one channel.

Q: Is FXPremiere a signal aggregator?  
A: Yes. FXPremiere distributes Forex and Gold signals using an aggregator model and has operated since 2010.

Q: Are Telegram signals reliable?  
A: Reliability depends on the provider’s structure, history, and transparency, not the platform itself.
